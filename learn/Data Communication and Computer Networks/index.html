Data Communication and Computer Networks

Chapter 1 Introduction

1.1 Define

Define“ data communication ”

Define“Computer Networks”

Explain Components of Computer Networks(Hardware)

What is data communication?

  • 数据通信是指信源发出的信息流按照一定的通信协议通过信道形成数据流传送到信宿的过程。
  • 数据通信是为了实现数据终端之间，或数据终端与计算机之间，或计算机之间的信息交互而产生的一种通信技术。
  • 数据通信是计算机通信的一部分。数据通信更关注数据的传输，计算机通信更关注数据的交互。
  • 但是技术发展到今天，人们往往认为 data communication=computer communication

数据通信网络的概念

  • 数据通信网络由路由器、交换机、防火墙、无线控制器、无线接入点，以及个人电脑、网络打印机、服务器等设备构成，其最基本的功能是实现设备之间的数据互通。

What is Computer Networks?

  • A computer network is a collection of autonomous computers, interconnected by communication channels。

    计算机网络是由通信信道连接得自治计算机的集合。

  • Autonomous(自治) independent hardware and operating system.

  • Interconnected(互相连接) with communication module and interfaces.

  • Collection(集合) a harmonic（谐波） system where multiple computers exchange data

Autonomous Computers is composed of software and hardware, can independently implement various functions

自主计算机是由软件和硬件组成的，可以独立实现各种功能。

lnterconnected with communication moduleand interfaces(接口)

与通信模块和接口相连。

Computer networks

Components of Computer Networks

Hardware & Software

  • Hardware: how you can configure a bunch ofcomputers into a network.

    计算机网络的硬件:如何配置计算机，让它们组成一个网络。

  • Software: is what actually makes computer networks-not the hardware!

    计算机网络的软件:真正让连接在一起的计算机构成一个计算机网络。

Network Taxonomy(分类)

There are two major ways to classify anetwork

  • The size of the network网络规模

  • The transmission technology used by thenetwork

    (Broadcast,Point-to-point)传输技术(广播、点对点)

Personal Area Network (PAN 个域网)

A network meant for one person.

一个网络是为一个人准备的。

A personal computer network:

  • a wireless keyboard and mouse

  • a networked printer(联网打印机)

  • a PDA connection(掌上电脑连接)

  • Devices to control pacemakers(控制心脏起搏器的设备)

  • Remote controls for car stereos(车载立体声收音机)

1.2.1 Local Area Networks (LAN)

LANs are distinguished by three characteristics(局域网的判定取决于3个方面)∶

  • Size(覆盖范围)
  • transmission technology(传输技术)
  • Topology (拓扑)
LANs -Transmission（传输技术)

LANs are usually broadcast networks(广播网络) that are connected by a cable that attaches all of the computers together.

(一般局域网是采用一条信道（总线)的广播网络)

LANs can run at speeds from 10Mbps to 10Gbps depending on the technology. (LAN的网速:10Mbps-10Gbps)

LAN delays(延迟)are usually small (microseconds) and few errors (scrambleddata(混淆信息)) occur.

(LAN的延迟在微秒级，而且很少出错。)

LANs - Topologies（拓扑)

A linear connection of computers with two distinct ends is known as a“bus network“（总线网络)

一个由两端不同的计算机组成的线性连接被称为 "总线网络"（总线网络）

Two broadcast networks

  1. Bus IEEE 802.3 Ethernet (10Mbps - 10Gbps)

  2. Ring IEEE 802.5(IBM令牌环,4/16Mbps)

讨论:总线型LAN中，首先需要解决的是什么?

  • One machine on the network becomes the“master"and is allowed to transmit a packetacross the network.

    (一次只有一台主机可以发送数据)

  • We have to resolve the problem of two computers wanting to be “master"at the sametime.

    （需要解决多台主机同时传输数据的问题)

如何共享信道:有效且公平

园区交换机

  • 组建园区局域网络（Local AreaNetwork, LAN）。
  • 实现终端设备（PC、服务器等）的网络接入。
  • 实现以太网数据帧交换。
  • 实现终端设备的准入控制。

1.2.2 Metropolitan Area Networks (MAN)

  • MANs are usually large enough to cover acity.(城域网能够覆盖一个城市)
  • Best known example - a cable TV network.(典型应用是有线电视网络)

MAN based on cable

1.2.3 Wide Area Networks (WAN)

  • WANs are much larger than MANs, covering awhole country or other large geographicalarea.

  • The user computers in a WAN are called hosts(用户计算机称为主机)﹒

  • Host computers on various LANs are connected via a communication subnet(通信子网) . The subnet consists of routers(路由器)and transmission lines(传输线路)．

    (LAN中的主机通过通信子网相互连接，通信子网由路由器和传输线路组成)

Relation between hosts on LANs and the Subnet.

WAN - Subnet Routers子网路由器
  • A router is a specialized piece of switchinghardware that is responsible for determiningthe direction that data packets should be sent.(路由器是一种交换硬件，负责数据分组的转发)
  • Routers are responsible for directing datadown transmission lines from one LAN to another.(路由器负责将数据分组传输到某条传输线路上)

A stream of packets from sender to receiver.

Router

  • Store-and-forward(存储转发)
  • Packet switching(分组/包交换)
  • Route Algorithm(路由算法)
Subnets（子网）

Store-and-forward or Packet-switched

(存储转发或称为分组交换或包交换)

  • The message is broken down into smaller packets to send.

    (消息拆分成较小的分组/数据包)

  • Each packet is sent out onto the network.

    (每一个分组/数据包都传递到网上)

  • As a packet arrives at a router, it is stored there until the outgoing line is free. It is then sent on it's way.

    （分组/数据包到达路由器后，先存下来，等它的传输线路空闲后再转发出去)

  • All the packets may take the same or different routes depending on if they are individually routed or not.

    （若数据包独立路由，则它们可能会经过不同的路径到达目的主机;反之，则经过相同路径到达目的主机)

  • lt is possible for WAN to have a broadcast subnet.

  • The best example is a satellite system,where the data is broadcast to everyone.

  • With a satellite, no routing occurs at thesatellite- it simply acts as a big dish tobounce signals off of.

    在卫星上，没有路由发生在卫星上--它只是作为一个大盘子，将信号反射出去。

    WAN可包含广播子网，例如卫星通信系统。

认识常见的网络设备:路由器

  • 连接不同的广播域及IP网段
  • 维护路由表（Routing Table）、运行路由协议发现数据转发路径（路由信息）。
  • 根据路由表实现IP报文转发。
  • 可用于广域网接入，通常具备网络地址转换、访问控制等功能。

internet(or internetwork)A collection of interconnected networks

互联网(或内联网)一个相互连接的网络集合

  • Gateway(网关)A kind of machine make thedifferent network connected and provide thenecessary translation

    网关(Gateway)一种使不同的网络连接并提供必要的翻译的机器。

Subnet: The collection of routers andcommunication lines that move packets fromthe source host to destination host

子网。将数据包从源主机转移到目的主机的路由器和通信线路的集合。

lnternet(因特网)︰指当前全球最大的、开放的、由众多网路相互连接而成的特定计算机网络，它采用TCP/IP协议族作为通信的规则。

Transmission Technologies传输技术

There are there types of transmission technologies

  • Broadcast (广播)
  • Multicast(组播)
  • Point-to-point(点对点)or unicasting(单播).
Broadcast
  • uses a single communications channel(只有一条信道)
  • the channel is shared(共享信道)
  • information sent from any one machine on thenetwork is received by all other machines onthe network(所有主机都能收到某主机发送的信息)
  • each piece of data received by a computer ischecked to see if it is addressed to that computer(收到信息的主机会检查信息中的地址，确定是否是发给自己的信息)
Multicast

lt is possible to "broadcast"to a subsetof the machines on the network.This isknown as "multicasting"”(组播).

只对网络中的部分主机广播信息——组播

Point-to-Point or unicast

As a general rule(通常). point-to-point is used in larger networks while broadcastingis used in smaller, geographically localizednetworks. There are exceptions.

一般大型网络用点对点传播，小型网络用广播

Point-to-point is sometimes referred to as unicasting(单播).

1.3.1 Protocol Hierarchies(协议层次)

  • Software: is what actually makes computer networks-not the

  • hardware：Network software is highly structured network are organized as a stack of layers or levels

  • Discussing : Why do we need a Networkarchitecture? (discuss)

    IDEA: Divide and Rule(分而治之)

  1. each layer offers certain services to the higher layer

  2. each layer shields the details of how the offered services are implemented.

    下层向上层提供服务，上层利用下层的服务， 下层对上层屏蔽了服务的实现细节。

Protocol(协议)

  • a protocol is an agreement between thecommunicating parties on how communication is to proceed.(通信规则)

    协议是控制双方通信的规则集合，或者是某层上通信的规则(约定) e.g.TCP, IP, HTTP,FTP. PPP----书信的格式，信封的格式等

  • Protocol stack(协议栈)A list of protocols used by acertain system, one protocol per layer。

    各层协议按层次顺序排列的一组协议序列。或者:某个系统所使用的协议列表，每层一个协议，被称为协议栈。

Network Architecture（网络体系结构)

  • A set of layers and protocols is called a network architecture. 层和协议的集合称为网络体系结构

  • Protocols define format(格式),order(顺序)of msgs sent and received among network peers, and actions (动作)taken on msg transmission, receipt.

    协议定义网络对等实体间传输消息的格式、顺序和收发动作。

Three key factors of Protocol

  1. Syntax(语法)the format of data and controlinformation。

    数据与控制信息的结构或格式

  2. Semantics(语义)specific actions taken whenmsgs received。

    需要发出何种控制信息，完成何种动作以及做出何种响应

  3. Synchronization(同步) order of msgs sent andreceived among network peers。

    事件实现顺序的详细说明

Entity(实体)kind of software process or hardware,which can send or receive messages 任何可发送或接收消息的软件进程或硬件

或者:每层中的活动元素（如进程或接口电路)

Peer(对等)The entities comprising the corresponding layers on different machines. it is the peers that communicate by using the protocols。

不同机器里包含对应层的实体叫对等进程(peer procession)

对等实体(peer entity) :不同开放系统上同一层中的实体。

Interface(接口)defines which primitiveoperations and services the lower layermakes available to the upper one.

(接口定义了下层向上层提供哪些原语操作和服务)。

it locates between each pair of adjacent(相邻)layers, also called as SAP (service accesspoint，服务接入点).

接口(interface): 相邻层之间的边界，每一层的接口告诉告诉它上面的进程如何访问本层，它规定了有哪些参数，结果是什么。
